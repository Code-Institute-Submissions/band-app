Overview

Liz Seaver is a 26 year-old singer-songwriter from Skerries, Co.Dublin. Liz was most recently shortlisted for the 2012 RTE / RAAP Breakthrough Bursary Award and performed in a live concert on John Creedon's show. This summer, Liz has played The Castlepalooza Festival in Tullamore on RTE Radio 1's The John Murray Stage, where he picked twelve acts to play live from over 700 applicants.

Also this year, Newstalk's Tom Dunne named Liz in his top 10 singer-songwriters to watch in 2012, with her song 'Inside Out', as featured on her current promo CD. This song also made the Semi-Finals of the YouBloom International song-writing competition in 2010, judged by Bob Geldof among others. Turbulent Bliss is Liz's second independent album which she has been recording and self-funding over the last few years. It is now completed and available for download.

After starting to write songs and play guitar at age 15, it wasn't long before her talent was noticed locally and she played support to Damien Dempsey near her home in the Millbank Theatre at still just 16 years old. Since then, over the years Liz has played most major venues in Dublin and beyond, in cities such as Paris and Hamburg whilst headlining her own shows back in Dublin in venues such as The Sugar Club and Whelan's. She has also played support along the way to the likes of Jack L, Iain Archer, Honor Heffernan and Albert Niland among others. Other collaborative projects over the years have included working in the studio with Donal Lunny.

Liz released her first studio album 'Til The Day Fades In at the age of 18, shortly after completing her Leaving Certificate in 2005. While studying for her degree in NCAD, Dublin, Liz continued to write, record and develop her own style of music, drawing on the influences of such artists as PJ Harvey, KT Tunstall and Gemma Hayes to create her own unique blend of acoustic alternative folk-rock, as well as heartfelt piano music. With the release of Turbulent Bliss, Liz aims to become a recognised female singer-songwriter similar to the that of Sheryl Crow and continue to entertain audiences everywhere with her uplifting vocals and exciting live shows.
